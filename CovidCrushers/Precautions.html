Covid-19 Precautions

This virus passes on through saliva,mucus through coughs and sneezes which can travel upto 3-5 feet. To prevent the spread of COVID-19:

  • Clean your hands often for 20 Seconds. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.
  • Use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol if soap and water are not available.
  • Maintain at least 1 meter (3 feet) distance between yourself and other people, particularly those who are coughing, sneezing and have a fever.
  • Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible.
  • Donâ€™t touch your eyes, nose or mouth.
  • Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell.
  • If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention.
  • Calling in advance allows your healthcare provider to quickly direct you to the right health facility. This protects you, and prevents the spread of viruses and other infections.
  • Masks can help prevent the spread of the virus from the person wearing the mask to others. Masks alone do not protect against COVID-19, and should be combined with physical distancing and hand hygiene. Follow the advice provided by your local health authority.
  • Avoid close contact with those sick.
  • Regularly clean doorknobs,handles,light switches,tables with soap and water.
  • Avoid sharing foods or drinks to others and don't share face masks.
  • If you share a bathrooms with someone, regular cleaning and separate towels are must.
  • Always check local guidelines before planning your event.
  • Stay home and stay safe as much as possible.
